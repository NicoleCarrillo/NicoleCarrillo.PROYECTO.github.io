Solution 1
Explanation
We develop a prototype of a videogame for children, which consists of a character that is swimming and has to catch all the bottles that fall into the ocean, while it have to avoid touching the fish. If he does not collect all the bottles on time, then the character sinks. The decision to make a game was because children are now using technology since an early age, if instead of playing a game like Clash Royale, they play an educational game, they will start generating an idea about what is correct to do.

Impact
The impact in children that we expect with our game , is to generate awareness through a simple game where you must collect garbage. The game allows children and adolescents to develop their learning in a fun way while they build, imitate and create. After several studies and discussions, it has been concluded that the proper use of video games can enhance the intellectual and emotional development of this group, while they can be a fun way in which parents and children enjoy of these new technologies and time together. In this way when a child after playing our game for a while, then when going to the beach and see the bottles thrown away, they will automatically pick it up.
Expert Feedback
"I think it's an interesting idea, but to avoid it being a simple game and the children stay with that idea, you can add a story where the current situation is explained or something a little with more educational value." -Noah Johnson
First versions
Our first version instead of being a person our main character, was a shark. But this could give the impression that the shark eats the garbage. This is bad because it did not generate the idea that we wanted to leave in children. Also points were given each time you picked up a garbage and the bigger the garbage, the more points we gave them. Our prototype has not yet implemented this but in the future we will add it.
User Feedback
