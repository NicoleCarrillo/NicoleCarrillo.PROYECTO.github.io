PERSONA

 The audience centers on those who are affected by the main problem we’re targeting although we are also going for young people, because they’re the future of our planet and if we do not work towards a better life through caring about our planet, we won’t be able to survive long. Our Persona is Diego Farias a hardworking fisherman, who likes to work in favor of his community and obviously, his family; he wants to accomplish his everyday goals which are working hard and trying to get his children future secured. He has 2 major problems regarding to his work, poaching and pollution, those are a major issue all through the Zihuantanejo coast in which he lives, Diego hopes these problems solve soon so he can keep working to maintain a good life standard for his loved ones.
